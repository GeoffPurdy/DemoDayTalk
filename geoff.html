Word Lattice

 Word Ladder: Invented by Lewis Carrol on Christmas Day in 1877. Published in Vanity Fair in 1879. Change one word into another by single letter changes forming existing words. Neo4j Graph database. Computed all single letter changes relationships for ~35,000 words two to twenty-two characters in length. Cypher query finds paths between words.
